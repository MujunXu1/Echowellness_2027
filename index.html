Mission

Student-led fundraising with a clear purpose.

EchoWellness is a fully student-run organization that creates fundraising events for community causes.

Members plan, perform, and lead each campaign while building a supportive environment for students and families.

Journey School

A fundraiser for Journey School.

Journey School serves uniquely gifted and talented students in the Greater Houston area. This campaign supports resources that help students learn, grow, and build independence.
