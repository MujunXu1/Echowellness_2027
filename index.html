Who We Are

A student-run organization centered on wellness, leadership, and service.

We create events that give students real responsibility and give communities meaningful support.

EchoWellness gives students a platform to lead meaningful projects, bring people together, and support organizations doing important work.

Our work combines fundraising, student performances, outreach, and community partnerships so every campaign has a clear purpose.
