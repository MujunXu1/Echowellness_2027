Executive President

Ayan Shashidhar is the Executive President of EchoWellness, where he channels his passion for leadership and community development into student-run philanthropic projects. Outside of his role in wellness advocacy, Ayan is highly involved in community service, youth instruction, and martial arts, where he holds a third-degree black belt. An avid tech enthusiast, he also spends his time coding, designing websites, and teaching student programming workshops.
